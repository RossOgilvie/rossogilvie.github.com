Method of characteristics for an Inhomogeneous PDE Use the method of characteristics to solve the following inhomogeneous PDE
x∂xu + y∂yu = 1

on the domain x > 0,y ∈ ℝ, with initial condition u(1,y) = y. Note, the function u will not be constant along the characteristic, but its value along the characteristic will be determined by its initial value. (6 points)
